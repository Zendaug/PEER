Uploading the template to LimeSurvey
--

1. Sign into LimeSurvey using your credentials.
2. Click on the "Create a new survey" option.
3. Choose the "Import" tab.
4. Click on the "Choose file" button.
5. Choose the "Peer_Evaluation_Survey_Template.lss" using the file picker.
6. Click on the "Import Survey" button, and then "Go to survey".

A new survey will be created from the template. The template is based on the Comprehensive Assessment of Team Member Effectiveness by Ohland et al. (2012):

Ohland, M. W., Loughry, M. L., Woehr, D. J., Bullard, L. G., Felder, R. M., Finelli, C. J., Layton, R. A., Pomeranz, H. R., & Schmucker, D. G. (2012). The comprehensive assessment of team member effectiveness: Development of a behaviorally anchored rating scale for self- and peer evaluation. Academy of Management Learning & Education, 11(4), 609-630. https://doi.org/10.5465/amle.2010.0177 




Creating a new list of survey participants
--
1. In LimeSurvey, click on "Settings" button and then "Survey participants".
2. If prompted, choose the "Switch to closed-access mode".
3. On the next screen, click Create -> Import participants from CSV file
4. Click on the "Choose file" button, and use the file picker to select the Contacts.csv file createed by PEER (from the "Create Contacts list from group membership" option.
5. Click on the "Upload" button.


Creating a distribution list (to send out unique survey links using PEER)
--
1. Go to your survey and click on "Survey Participants".
2. Click on the "Generate tokens" button, and select "Yes" and "OK".
3. Click on the "Export" button.
4. Click the green "Download CSV file" button.

A .CSV file will download on to your computer. Use this file in the "Send survey invitations from distribution list" feature of PEER.

Note: In the bulk message, you will need to replace the text "(obtain this link from LimeSurvey)" with the web address for the survey. You can obtain this link from the Settings -> Overview screen, within the "Share your survey" box.
